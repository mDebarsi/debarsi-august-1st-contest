The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhounnd. The Beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the Beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricuture imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The Beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
